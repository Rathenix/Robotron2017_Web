Make sure to play in wondowed 1024x768
Press ESC to quit from the main menu
WASD to move
Arrows to shoot
You can shoot two ways at once
You're the blue guy
Kill and avoid red guys and their bullets
ESC in-game to toggle pause
Red guys are worth 50 points
Red bullets are worth 10 points
You lose 5 points per second
Kill all red guys to advance the level
You are invulnerable for 1 second at the start of each level
Each level has 1 more enemy
Enemies have a speed boost every 2 levels
Enemies shoot faster every 3 levels
Don't break the highscores.txt file
